Welcome to Popdeem.

The Popdeem iOS SDK allows to to leverage the power of the Popdeem Social Marketing platform from within your existing iOS app. To get started, download the SDK via cocoa pods (pod 'PopdeemSDK'), or add the source to your project. Then head on over to our developer portal for in-depth instructions to get started.
